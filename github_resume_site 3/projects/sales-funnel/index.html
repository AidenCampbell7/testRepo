Sales Funnel Analyzer

Use this tool to model the stages of your sales funnel and quickly uncover bottlenecks. Enter the number of prospects at each stage (from leads to closed deals) and the average deal value; the analyzer will compute conversion rates, estimate total revenue and identify the stage with the greatest drop‑off. A horizontal bar chart visualises the pipeline, and a summary below breaks down each metric so you can focus your efforts where they matter most.
