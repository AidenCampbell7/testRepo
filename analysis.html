SoundHound AI Company Analysis

SoundHound AI, Inc. develops voice and conversational AI technologies used across automotive, restaurant, retail, financial and healthcare industries. Following its public listing via a SPAC in 2021, the company has focused on expanding its customer base and product offerings, leading to rapid revenue growth but widening losses as it invests in scaling its platform.
